 Our event-study analyses are indexed by event time relative to the first CHF hospitalization , which we treat as a plausibly exogenous clinical shock. Because NPCP enrollment may occur either before or after hospitalization, some periods fall between the two dates. In those intervals, treatment status relative to hospitalization is ambiguous.
