 In this section, we develop SSTL, a synchronous variant of STL designed specifically for CPS that operate under discrete time. Our approach is to extend STL for signals obtained through synchronous execution of physical systems, which we call SSTL. In SSTL, time progresses in discrete time instants or ticks, and traces are obtained at tick boundaries. Since most physical signals are continuous and real-valued over ℝ ≥ 0 \mathbb{R}_{\geq 0} , to validate using SSTL, we need to project the real-time variable t t to a discrete time variable [ t ] [t] . This projection, combined with our SIH, ensures that we can reason about discrete-time behaviour while maintaining soundness with respect to the underlying continuous system.
